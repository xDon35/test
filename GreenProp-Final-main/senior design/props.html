Heat Keep Playoff Hopes Alive with Win over Nets

The Miami Heat kept their playoff hopes alive with a 113-107 victory over the Brooklyn Nets on Tuesday night. The Heat were led by Jimmy Butler, who scored a game-high 33 points and added 10 rebounds and 8 assists. Bam Adebayo also had a big game for Miami, finishing with 22 points and 10 rebounds.

Lakers Clinch Playoff Berth with Win over Timberwolves

The Los Angeles Lakers clinched a playoff berth with a 124-106 win over the Minnesota Timberwolves on Wednesday night. LeBron James led the way for the Lakers with 25 points, 12 rebounds, and 6 assists, while Anthony Davis added 20 points and 8 rebounds. The win secured the Lakers' spot in the postseason for the third straight year.
